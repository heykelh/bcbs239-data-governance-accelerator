# Business Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Customer ID | Unique identifier of a banking customer |
| Transaction ID | Unique identifier of a financial transaction |
| Exposure Amount | Financial exposure associated with a transaction, account or portfolio |
| Booking Date | Date when a transaction is recorded in a source system |
| Reporting Date | Date used for risk or management reporting |
| Risk Segment | Business classification used for risk monitoring |
| Critical Data Element | Data element required for important operational or regulatory processes |

## Purpose

The glossary helps align business and technical stakeholders around shared definitions for critical data elements.
